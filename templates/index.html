Mathematical Notes

Boole's Rule is a 4th-order Newton-Cotes formula used to approximate definite integrals using 5 points.

  • Formula: \( \frac{2h}{45} [7f_0 + 32f_1 + 12f_2 + 32f_3 + 7f_4] \)
  • h Value: \( (b-a)/4 \)
  • Accuracy: Exact for polynomials of degree \(\leq 5\).
